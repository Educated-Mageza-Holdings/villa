Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days across South Africa. Express delivery options are also available at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging for a full refund or exchange.

Are your perfumes authentic?

Yes! All our fragrances are 100% authentic and sourced from reputable suppliers. We stand behind the quality of every product.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale options for bulk orders. Please contact us for more information about wholesale pricing and minimum order quantities.

How long do the perfumes last?

Our perfumes are designed to be long-lasting, typically lasting 6-8 hours or more depending on the fragrance and your skin type.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email so you can monitor your delivery.
